Features

Top-Notch Security

Here in Sky Notes, your password and your notes is securely kept in our database. With the use of PHP's BCrypt, your password will be never known by anyone!

Easy To Navigate UI

Browsing through your notes has never been easy until you stumbled in this web application. With clear and concise buttons and its satisfying look, you can never go back to old ways!

Theme Preference

Do your eyes hurt while looking at your screen? Here in Sky Notes, we have a dark mode to cater people who don't want the blinding look of light mode! You can also switch it back to light mode if you take your notes with many light source around you!

Experience The Demo

Want to test it out before actually registering an account? You can click the image beside this text to go to the demo site of Sky Notes! Take note that the data you'll put in the demo site will be gone once you closed it. Be careful!
